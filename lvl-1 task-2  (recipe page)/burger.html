Burger

Burger

Time: 15 min

How to Prepare

    1. Prepare the Patty:

  • 01.

    Combine Ingredients:
    - In a bowl, mix the ground beef with salt, black pepper, and any optional seasonings (like garlic powder, onion powder, or paprika) until evenly combined.

  • 02.

    Form Patties:
    - Divide the mixture into equal portions and shape each portion into a patty, about 3/4 inch thick. Make a slight indentation in the center of each patty to prevent them from puffing up while cooking.

    • 2. Cook the Patties:

  • 03.

    Preheat:
    - Preheat a grill, skillet, or frying pan over medium-high heat. If using a skillet or pan, add a little oil to prevent sticking.

  • 04.

    Cook:
    - Place the patties on the hot surface. Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side for medium doneness, or adjust the time based on your preferred level of doneness. Avoid pressing down on the patties with a spatula, as this can release juices and make the burgers dry.

  • 05.

    Add Cheese:
    - If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking. Cover the grill or pan to melt the cheese.

    • 3. Toast the Buns:

  • 06.

    Butter and Toast:
    - Spread a thin layer of butter on the cut sides of the hamburger buns. Place the buns, buttered side down, on the grill or in a separate pan. Toast until golden brown, about 1-2 minutes.

    • 4. Assemble the Burger:

  • 07.

    Layer the Ingredients:
    - Spread condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise) on the bottom half of the toasted buns.
    - Add lettuce, tomato slices, and onion slices.
    - Place the cooked patty with melted cheese on top of the vegetables.
    - Add pickles and any other desired toppings (bacon, avocado, jalapeños, mushrooms, fried egg, BBQ sauce).

  • 08.

    Top it Off:
    - Cover with the top half of the bun.

    • 5. Serve:

  • 09.

    - Serve the assembled burgers immediately with your favorite sides like fries, salad, or coleslaw. Enjoy!

Ingredients

    For the Patty:

  1. Ground beef (or alternative protein like chicken, turkey, or plant-based options)
  2. Salt
  3. Black pepper
  4. Optional seasonings (garlic powder, onion powder, paprika)

    5. For the Bun:

  5. Hamburger buns (sesame, brioche, whole wheat, etc.)

    6. For Assembly:

  6. Cheese slices (cheddar, American, Swiss, etc.)
  7. Lettuce leaves
  8. Tomato slices
  9. Onion slices (red or white)
  10. Pickles
  11. Ketchup
  12. Mustard
  13. Mayonnaise

    14. Optional Toppings:

  14. Bacon
  15. Avocado slices
  16. Jalapeños
  17. Mushrooms
  18. Fried egg
  19. BBQ sauce

    20. Additional Ingredients:

  20. Butter (for toasting the buns)
  21. Oil (for cooking the patty)
