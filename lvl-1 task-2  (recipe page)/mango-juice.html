Mango Juice

Mango Juice

Time: 15 min

How to Prepare

    1. Prepare the Mangoes:

  • 01.

    Peel and Chop:
    - Rinse 2 cups of sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear.

    • 2. Blend the Ingredients:

  • 02.

    Add Mangoes to Blender:
    - Place the chopped mango pieces into a blender.

  • 03.

    Add Water:
    - Pour in 1 to 1.5 cups of water, adjusting the amount based on your desired juice consistency. Start with 1 cup and add more if needed.

  • 04.

    Sweeten (Optional):
    - If the mangoes are not sweet enough, add sugar or honey to taste. Start with 1-2 tablespoons and adjust based on your preference.

  • 05.

    Add Lemon Juice (Optional):
    - Add a tablespoon of lemon juice for a hint of tanginess, if desired.

    • 3. Blend:

  • 06.

    Blend Until Smooth:
    - Blend all the ingredients until you get a smooth, homogenous mixture.

  • 07.

    Check Consistency:
    - If the juice is too thick, add a little more water and blend again.

    • 4. Serve:

  • 07.

    Strain (Optional):
    - If you prefer a smoother juice, strain the mixture through a fine mesh sieve to remove any fibrous bits.

  • 08.

    Add Ice (Optional):
    - Pour the juice into glasses over ice cubes for a chilled version.

  • 08.

    Garnish (Optional):
    - Garnish with a slice of mango or a mint leaf for presentation.

  • 09.

    Enjoy:
    - Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing mango juice!

Ingredients

    Ingredients for Mango Juice:

  1. Ripe mangoes (2-3 medium-sized, peeled and chopped)
  2. Water (1 to 1.5 cups, adjust based on desired consistency)
  3. Sugar or honey (optional, to taste)
  4. Lemon juice (optional, for a hint of tanginess)
  5. Ice cubes (optional, for serving)
